Products

Here are some of the products we offer

Edible Copra

Copra is valued for the coconut oil extracted from it and for the resulting residue, coconut-oil cake, which is used mostly for livestock feed. Copra. Copra was introduced as a source of edible fat in northern Europe in the 1860s because of a shortage of dairy fats. Early in the 20th century it became known in the United States.

Semi Husked Coconuts

Our organization offers a wide variety of Semi Husked Coconuts. The offered range of coconuts is highly acclaimed for its qualitative taste and purity. In fact, this coconut is also known for the highest value of vitamins, proteins and other nutrients.

Coconut shell charcoal

Coconut shell charcoal is a by-product of a pyrolysis process which involves burning coconut shells in a limited supply of oxygen to carbonize them first. After that, the coconut shell charcoal is ground into a powder and mixed with glue to make various shapes and sizes of charcoal briquettes. Drying and packaging are the last steps that must be carried out before the launch of coconut shell charcoal.

Coco Fiber

Coco fiber is one of the components of coconut fruit which is processed and decomposed can become a product needed for the domestic market and even become a high export value product. Coco fiber and coco peat are two derivative products of coconut coir that can be processed into interior supporting materials or furniture and planting media through several studies.
